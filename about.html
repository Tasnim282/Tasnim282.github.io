About Hotel

Located along a busy road, this apartment hotel with a friendly atmosphere is 11 km from Bizerte Airport and less than 1 km from the historic Fort d'Espagne, offering panoramic views of the city of Bizerte.

Simply furnished, accommodation ranges from studios, to apartments and 3-bedroom duplexes. They are equipped with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and a kitchenette. They also have a balcony and a safe. The duplexes also have a second floor and a second shower.
